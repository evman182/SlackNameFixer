Privacy Policy

What we collect

We collect and store the list of Slack workspace members who are authorized with the app, as well as the preferred name set by the member, and a token to allow updating the member's profile. No other data about members is stored.

The data is stored safely in Microsoft Azure.

What we share

The only data shared outside our servers is the preferred name set by the user, as this is sent to Slack's API when updating the user's profile.

No other data is shared with any other service.

Privacy policy changes

We may change our Privacy Policy from time to time. Your further use of the Services after a change to our Privacy Policy will be subject to the updated policy
